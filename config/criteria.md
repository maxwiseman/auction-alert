# Auction taste criteria

Pick classic or emerging-classic Sunday cars that are fun to drive.

Strong positives:

- Lightweight or compact
- Manual transmission
- Driver quality
- Interesting spec, color, engine, or ownership story
- Likely to end under $25,000

Avoid:

- Major rust
- Salvage titles
- Vague listings
- Heavy luxury cars
- Large SUVs
- Obvious projects
- Museum pieces that are too nice to drive
